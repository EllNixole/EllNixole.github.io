About

Hello, I'm Dani, the passionate baker and creative mind behind Cakes By Dani! Baking and decorating cakes have been my lifelong passions, and I'm thrilled to share my sweet creations with you. As a one-person business, every cake that leaves my kitchen is crafted with love and dedication, ensuring that you receive not just a cake, but a work of art that adds a touch of magic to your special moments.

My journey into the world of baking began as a hobby, baking treats for family and friends. Their encouragement and delight in my creations inspired me to turn my passion into a small business.

Your celebration is unique, and I'm here to create a cake that perfectly complements your special occasion. Whether it's a whimsical birthday cake or a delightful anniversary surprise, I love collaborating with you to bring your vision to life. Your joy and satisfaction are what fuel my creativity!
